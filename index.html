The ZYRANA Story

Crafting Emotions Through Scent

Founded in the heart of artisan perfumery, ZYRANA was born from a passion to create fragrances that tell a story. Our master perfumers blend time-honored techniques with innovative approaches to craft scents that transcend the ordinary.

Each ZYRANA fragrance is meticulously composed using the finest ingredients sourced from around the world. From the delicate florals of Grasse to exotic spices of the East, we select only premium components that meet our exacting standards.

Our commitment to sustainable luxury means we create beautiful scents with minimal environmental impact, using ethically sourced ingredients and eco-conscious packaging.

Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Information

At ZYRANA, we aim to deliver your luxury fragrances as quickly and safely as possible.

Delivery Options

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days (Free on orders over $100)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days ($15)
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days (Fees vary by location)

Shipping Restrictions

Due to regulations regarding alcohol-based fragrances, we are unable to ship to certain countries. Please contact our customer service team for details about your specific location.

Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your ZYRANA purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer a straightforward returns process.

Return Conditions

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery
  • Products must be in original, unopened packaging
  • Gift sets must be returned complete
  • Return shipping fees are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error

How to Initiate a Return

To begin the return process, please contact our customer service team at returns@zyrana.com with your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with a return authorization and detailed instructions.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, your refund will be processed within 7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Special Orders & Custom Fragrances

Custom-blended fragrances and special orders are non-refundable due to their personalized nature. Please contact us with any concerns about your custom order.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our shipping or returns policies, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team at inquiries@zyrana.com or call us at +1 (800) 555-SCENT.
